# Product Overview

DerHex is a decentralized launchpad platform that enables users to participate in blockchain-based investment opportunities and DeFi activities.

## Core Features

- **Launchpad/IDOs**: Discover and invest in early-stage blockchain projects through Initial DEX Offerings
- **Staking**: Multiple staking pool options with competitive APY rates and lock periods
- **Bonds**: Fixed-term investment opportunities with guaranteed returns
- **Giveaways/Airdrops**: Community rewards and engagement opportunities
- **Governance**: Platform voting system with rewards for participation
- **Dynamic Rewards**: Lock staking with tiered reward structures
- **Seasonal Staking**: Time-limited staking campaigns with special rewards
- **Leaderboard**: Track top participants and community engagement

## Supported Networks

- Base Sepolia (Chain ID: 84532)
- Sonic Testnet (Chain ID: 57054)
- Rise Testnet (Chain ID: 11155931) - Default network

## User Roles

- **Regular Users**: Participate in IDOs, stake tokens, vote on proposals, claim rewards
- **Admin Users**: Create and manage presales, bonds, giveaways, and voting proposals
- **Project Creators**: Launch their own presales and manage fundraising

## Key User Flows

1. Connect wallet via Privy authentication
2. Select blockchain network
3. Browse available deals (IDOs, bonds, giveaways)
4. Participate by staking, purchasing, or voting
5. Track investments and rewards in dashboard
6. Claim rewards and manage positions
